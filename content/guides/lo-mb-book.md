## I found out how to make a multi-book book...

## and the only ten-page limit rumor was half a lie!!!!!

1. Limit of five hundred pages, four starting/middle repeated blank
pages and ten repeated blank end pages max
2. Combine all books into one book
3. Remove all title/table of contents/dedication/hints/copyright/author/
promos/index sections from all books
4. Make a new set of those sections, not copies, nor for each book, 
FOR THE WHOLE BOOK, ONCE!!!!!
5. Replace all book headers of each book with the new one for the 
whole book
6. Keep the subtitle/leftover book format sections
7. Add two blank pages after each book
8. Format, as you please
9. Create a new series for the collection
10, VITAL--DON'T ADD THE COLLECTION TO ANY INDIVIDUAL BOOK'S SERIES!!!!!
11. Upload the book, with a price less than the one of all books,
combined, when bought individually, one by one
