How to make a page style in LibreOffice Writer:

1. In your document, got to the styles section and pick the icon whose,
hover info says page styles

2. Right click on the resulting section and pick new

3. Edit the new page style as you want

4. Pick ok

5. Save the document

6. Enjoy!
