# Bleed books versus no bleed books guide

Bleeding books:
-have pages, with ANYTHING, reaching the edges of the page
-have an added .125w x .25h dimension to them to cover the bleed
-completely bleeds
-has no white space on the edges

Non-bleeding books:
-have pages, with EVERYTHING within the margins of the page
-have ANYTHING in those pages, either cutoff, or limited by those
margins
-have an added .25-1 inch thick edges, ALL AROUND, keeping the book's
contents in those pages
-have white space on the edges

All book cover types:
-BLEED!!!!!
